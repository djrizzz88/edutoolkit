    What does this citation checker do?

    This tool helps students review references before submission. It does not replace official university guidance, but it can help spot common issues such as duplicates, missing dates, incomplete entries, and likely missing URL details.

    Why checking citations matters

    A strong reference list improves academic quality and reduces simple formatting mistakes. Clear and consistent citations are important in essays, reports, dissertations, and coursework.
