How this GPA calculator works

This calculator uses a weighted average method. Each module grade is multiplied by its credit value, then all weighted scores are added together and divided by the total number of credits.

  • Enter each module name for your own reference.
  • Enter the grade as a number, for example 68 or 72.
  • Enter the credit value for that module, for example 15 or 20.
  • Click calculate to get your overall average.

Who can use this tool?

This GPA calculator is useful for students in the UK and internationally. If your institution uses a different GPA conversion system, you can still use this page to calculate a weighted average and adapt it later.
